Facing
the Child

Workshop for Children

Bhoomika has experienced personnel who can conduct theatre workshops with children. We have worked with many schools and NGOs in this area. These workshops involve group activities and games which are sensitive to the child’s personality and behavior. They are designed and conducted in a way that will open up a shy child, teach a bully to work with a team, help a child to explore her creative skill. Conducted in an informal atmosphere, these workshops serve a generous helping of fun and frolic. Often, the icing on the cake is a complete play production, adding the extra dose of sweetness that always excites children. Most importantly, some of the teachers can be in the workshop as observers and in due course of time they can conduct workshops on their own.

Story Telling for Children

We at Bhoomika, specially create plays for children of different age groups addressing their concerns and questions. These professionally mounted plays are designed in such a way that they are interactive with the children and they can be staged in any space, without the need of a well-equipped stage. And most importantly the children would have lot of fun watching them. We specialise in engaging children with interactive story telling sessions with live sound effects.
